# 🧠 AI from the Ground Up

Welcome! This repo is where I’m documenting my journey of learning AI the hard way. 

Most people just "import" a library and call it a day, but I want to actually understand what’s happening under the hood. No high-level abstractions here—just the core fundamentals, the math, and the raw logic.

### 📍 Why I'm doing this
I’m following **Ayush Singh’s Roadmap** to stop being a "copy-paste" developer and start being someone who actually understands how these systems work. This is my personal lab for practice code, notes, and the resources that finally made things "click" for me.

### 📂 What you'll find here
* **My Practice Code:** Raw implementations of algorithms.
* **Cheatsheets:** Simple, plain-English notes on things like Linear Algebra and Calculus so I don't forget them.
* **Resources:** Links to the best videos and papers I’ve found that actually explain things well.

### 🛠 What I’m focusing on
* **The Math:** Linear Algebra, Probability, and Calculus (the stuff that actually powers AI).
* **Machine Learning:** Building models from scratch to see how they tick.
* **Deep Learning:** Understanding neural networks without just relying on a framework.
* **MLOps:** Moving code out of a messy notebook and into something that actually works in the real world.
